About Us

Brief History

JCCI Zion Chapel started in 2001 as a small Worship and Bible study group in a living room in Marks Gate, Dagenham. Since its inception the church experienced phenomenal growth, and has now grown to become a group of strong believers who come together to worship as one body in Christ.

Our Vision

Building a spiritual house for spiritual sacrifices Our Beliefs JCCI Zion Chapel accepts the sacred words of the 66 Books of the Bible as the inspired revelation of God, as the sheer anchor of Faith and Eternal Hope and as the perfect creed to the beliefs and deed for the believers and also for the general unity of the Church.

REASONS WHY YOU NEED JESUS

Jesus Loves you! He desires to have a relationship with you, to give you a life full of jo and purpose. Why do you need him in your life?


1You have a past........

You can't go back but he can. The bible says, "Jesus Christ the same yesterday today and forever" (Herbrew 13:8). He can walk into those places of sin and failure, wipe the slate clean and give you a new beginning.



2Because you need a friend

Jesus knows the worst of you, yet he believes in the best. Why? Because he see you not as you are, but as you will be when he gets through with you. What a friend!



3Because he holds the future

Who else are you going to trust? In his hands you are safe and secure- today, tomorrow, and for eternity. His word says "For I know the plans I have for you.... Plans for good and not for evil, to give you a future and a hope. In those days when you pray I will listen" (Jeremiah 26:11-13 TLB)
